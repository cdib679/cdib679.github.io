Biography

About Kasumi

Born in Bologna, Italy, Aria Rossi grew up surrounded by music in a family of passionate music lovers. She began vocal studies at the age of nine and was accepted to the Conservatorio G.B. Martini at seventeen, where she studied under Maestro Giovanni Ferretti for six formative years.

Her professional debut came in 2015 as Mimì in La Bohème at the Teatro Comunale di Bologna, drawing immediate critical attention for the warmth and purity of her tone. Since then she has gone on to perform leading roles across Italy, Austria, Germany, and the United Kingdom.

Equally at home in Verdi and Mozart, Aria brings a meticulous attention to text and dramatic intention that distinguishes her interpretations. She collaborates regularly with conductors such as and has appeared at festivals including Glyndebourne, Aix-en-Provence, and the Rossini Opera Festival.

In addition to her operatic career, she is a committed recitalist and recording artist. Her debut album, Canzoni d'Amore, was released in 2022 to widespread acclaim. She is represented by and managed by .
